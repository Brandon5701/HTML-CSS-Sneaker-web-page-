SneakerPlug.NYC

-Store Brand:SneakerPlug.NYC


-Description:A fashion-based sneaker store that sells the most popular name brand sneakers for retail prices with limited stock on each shoe. Each shoe will sell out in seconds online and when the sneaker store is put in a local area will be a huge line with raffles each time a big release drops. Brands such and Nike, Adidas, Jordan, and others will be sold. Collab releases with off-white, Palm, Angles, Fear of God, Kaws, Commes Des Garcon, Bape and many more.


-purpose: I am interested in this brand because it will bring more job to the community with selling products that will always be in demand. two to three stores located on local areas of Brooklyn, Queens, and Manhattan along with was a website will bring more than enough revenue and job opportunities to the community. In the future the brand will expand others places outside NYC such as Seattle, Los Angeles, Atlanta, Florida, and Washington D.C. The stores will have merchandise other than sneakers. Donations and food pantry will be held twice at the store to uplift the community. 2 percent of profit will be donated to the red cross and 3 percent will be donated to covid relief. Investing & funding SneakerPlug.NYC is a great choice because its an attraction that people cannot resist. Fashion sneakers are like the stocks because something is always on the rise. People love fashion and will do anything for it because it has the potential to bring different people together to celebrate their own individuality. Most people are attracted to fashion that is hard to find along with not easy to buy. In modern society we put fashion on a higher scale than its ever been. Some people find fashion as a better a better way to express their personality and SneakerPlug.NYC supports and understands. The goal is to bring positivity & income to the community after a chaotic year Due to Covid-19.
